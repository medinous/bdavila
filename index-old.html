About me

My name is Bettina, I'm Brazilian and I'm a product designer since 2014. I own a bachelor's degree in graphic design and a master's degree in social sciences. After I landed my first job as a web designer in 2012, I decided to dive in the product design field so I left Brazil to enjoy new professional opportunities in London, Munich, Paris and Lisbon. I've worked in early start-ups, non-profitable organisations and big unicorns, and in every step of the way I faced different problems and met people from - and within - many cultural contexts which helped me building my own skillset to create products the best way possible.
